Privacy Policy

  • 1. Information We Collect
    We only collect the information you voluntarily provide when using our services:
    Names: When using the name-based love calculator, you provide your first name and the first name of your partner.
    Zodiac Signs: When using the zodiac-based calculator, you provide your zodiac sign and your partner’s sign.
    We do not collect any other personal information such as email addresses, contact details, or any sensitive data.
  • 2. How We Use Your Information
    The information you provide (names and zodiac signs) is used solely for the purpose of calculating your love compatibility. We do not store, share, or use this information for any other purposes.
  • 3. Data Retention
    We do not retain any data provided during the use of our calculators. Once the calculation is completed and displayed, your information is automatically discarded. This means no personal data is stored on our servers.
  • 4. Cookies and Tracking
    Couple A More does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect your browsing data or track your activities on the site.
  • 5. Third-Party Services
    We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to any third-party services. Our website operates independently and does not use external data processors.
  • 6. Children’s Privacy
    Couple A More is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
  • 7. Security
    We take reasonable measures to protect your data during your interaction with our website. However, since we do not store or retain any of your personal information, the risks are minimal.
  • 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. We will post any changes on this page, and the effective date at the top will indicate when the latest version was implemented.
