About Couple A More

Welcome to Couple A More—your ultimate love compatibility tool! At Couple A More, we believe love can be explored through both the simplicity of names and the mystery of the stars. That’s why we offer two unique ways to calculate your romantic compatibility:

  • By Name: Curious about what your names reveal about your relationship? Simply enter both your names, and our algorithm will analyze the compatibility between the letters, vibrations, and hidden meanings in your names, delivering a love score that reflects your potential together.
  • By Zodiac: Prefer to look to the stars for answers? Our zodiac-based calculator uses your sun signs to uncover how well your astrological traits align. Discover how the elements—fire, water, air, and earth—interact between your signs and what that means for your connection. Whether you're a passionate Aries or a grounded Capricorn, see how your relationship shines based on astrology.

With Couple A More, love isn't just a number—it’s a cosmic and personal journey. Whether you choose to explore love through names or through the stars, we’re here to help you on your path to a deeper connection!
