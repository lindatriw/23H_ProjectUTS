Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. What is Couple A More?
    Couple A More is a love calculator website offering two unique ways to calculate relationship compatibility: by name and by zodiac. We combine name analysis and astrology to provide fun insights into your relationship.
  • 2. How does the name calculator work?
    Our name calculator analyzes the letters in your and your partner’s first names, looking for patterns that correspond to the symbolic meanings of each letter. We calculate compatibility based on how the names align and provide a love score.
  • 3. How does the zodiac calculator work?
    Our zodiac calculator uses your and your partner’s sun signs. By analyzing the elements (fire, water, air, earth) and astrological traits of each zodiac sign, we calculate how well the stars align in your relationship.
  • 4. Are the name and zodiac calculator results accurate?
    The results from Couple A More are meant for entertainment and offer a fun perspective on your relationship. While names and astrology carry symbolic meanings, these results should not be considered absolute truths in relationships.
  • 5. Is my data safe on the Couple A More website?
    We take your privacy seriously. The names and data you enter are not stored or used for any other purpose beyond the calculation.
